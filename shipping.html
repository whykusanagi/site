Shipping Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

At a glance: digital products are delivered electronically, physical products are made-to-order via Printify, and total delivery time is production time plus shipping time.

This Shipping Policy applies to physical products purchased through whykusanagi.xyz. Our merchandise is produced on-demand through our fulfillment partner, Printify, and shipped from their production facilities.

1. Digital Products (No Shipping)

Digital products are delivered instantly via download link.

  • Doujinshi PDFs, digital artwork, and e-books are NOT shipped
  • Download links are sent to your email immediately after payment
  • No shipping fees apply to digital products

See our Refund Policy for digital product terms.

2. Print-on-Demand Production

How It Works

Physical merchandise (apparel, accessories, prints) is custom-made after you order:

  1. Order Placed: You complete checkout on our Shopify store
  2. Production Starts: Order is sent to Printify's nearest production facility (2-7 days depending on location)
  3. Quality Check: Item is printed, assembled, and inspected
  4. Shipped: Package is handed to shipping carrier with tracking
  5. Delivered: Carrier delivers to your address

Why Print-on-Demand?

  • No Pre-Made Inventory: Items are made fresh for each order
  • Higher Quality: Each item is individually crafted
  • Sustainable: No waste from unsold stock
  • Longer Wait: Takes more time than pre-stocked items

3. Processing Times

Production Time (Before Shipping)

Allow 2-7 business days for production, depending on product type:

Product Type Production Time
T-Shirts, Hoodies, Apparel 2-5 business days
Posters, Prints, Art 2-4 business days
Accessories (mugs, towels, etc.) 3-7 business days
All-Over-Print Items 4-7 business days

What "Business Days" Means

  • Monday-Friday only (excludes weekends)
  • Excludes major holidays (Christmas, New Year's, etc.)
  • Orders placed on weekends start processing Monday

Delays

Production may take longer during:

  • High-volume sales periods (product launches, holidays)
  • Supply chain disruptions (material shortages)
  • Quality control issues requiring reprints

6. Tracking Your Order

Tracking Number

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Email Notification: "Your order has shipped" with tracking link
  • Tracking Number: Unique code to monitor your package
  • Carrier Website: Link to track on carrier's site (USPS, UPS, etc.)

Tracking Updates

Typical tracking milestones:

  1. "Label Created": Shipping label printed, package not yet picked up
  2. "In Transit": Package is moving through carrier's network
  3. "Out for Delivery": Package is on the delivery truck today
  4. "Delivered": Package was successfully delivered

Tracking Issues

  • No Updates for 3+ Days: Contact carrier first, then us if unresolved
  • "Tracking Not Found": Wait 24-48 hours; carrier systems update slowly
  • "Returned to Sender": Contact us immediately; may be address issue

Calculated at Checkout

Shipping fees are calculated based on:

Free Shipping

We occasionally offer free shipping promotions:

  • Check homepage for current offers

8. International Shipping Details

Customs & Duties

IMPORTANT: Customers are responsible for all customs fees, import duties, and taxes.

  • Fees are determined by your country's customs office
  • Amounts vary by country and product value
  • We cannot predict or control these charges
  • You must pay customs fees to receive your package
  • Refusal to pay = package returned = no refund

Customs Declaration

All international packages include customs forms declaring:

  • Product description (e.g., "T-shirt", "Poster")
  • Item value (purchase price)
  • Country of origin (where produced)

Prohibited Countries

We cannot ship to certain countries due to legal or logistical restrictions. Checkout will indicate if your country is unavailable.

9. Address & Delivery Issues

Correct Address Required

Double-check your address before ordering!

  • Include apartment/unit numbers
  • Use correct ZIP/postal codes
  • Ensure spelling is accurate

Address Errors

  • Before Shipment: Contact us immediately to update (may be too late if production started)
  • After Shipment: Contact the carrier to attempt address correction (additional fees may apply)
  • Returned to Sender: Customer pays reshipping fees for incorrect addresses

Delivery Locations

Carriers deliver to:

  • Residential addresses (front door, mailbox)
  • Business addresses (during business hours)
  • PO Boxes (USPS only; some items may not fit)
  • APO/FPO military addresses (domestic shipping rates)

Failed Delivery Attempts

If carrier cannot deliver (no one home, signature required):

  • Carrier will leave a notice with pickup instructions
  • Package may be held at local post office or carrier facility
  • You typically have 7-15 days to pick up before return to sender

10. Lost, Stolen, or Damaged Packages

Lost Packages

If tracking shows no updates for 10+ days after shipment:

  1. Contact the carrier first (they may locate it)
  2. If carrier confirms loss, contact us at support@whykusanagi.xyz
  3. We'll file a claim and send a replacement or refund

Stolen Packages (Porch Theft)

If tracking shows "Delivered" but you didn't receive it:

  1. Check with neighbors, building management, or household members
  2. Verify the address on your order confirmation
  3. File a police report for theft
  4. Contact us; we'll work with you on a case-by-case basis

Note: Once carrier marks "Delivered," it's out of our control, but we'll do our best to help.

Damaged in Transit

If your package arrives damaged:

  1. Take photos: Damaged packaging, damaged product, shipping label
  2. Contact us within 48 hours: support@whykusanagi.xyz
  3. Include: Order number, tracking number, photos
  4. Resolution: Free replacement or full refund
