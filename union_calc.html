Union Raid Boss HP Calculator

Use this calculator to estimate remaining boss HP after up to three player damage inputs. Select a level and boss, enter damage values, then run the calculation.

Data source: boss HP values are loaded from boss.json on this site.

How to Use

  1. Select the raid level and boss.
  2. Enter damage for Player 1 and Player 2.
  3. Enter Player 3 damage, or enable Ignore Player 3.
  4. Click Calculate to see remaining HP and bar visualization.

Quick FAQ

Q: Why does the level dropdown start at 1 even though internal values are zero-based?
A: The UI displays level +1 for readability while using the underlying dataset keys.

Q: Can this be used without a third player hit?
A: Yes. Enable Ignore Player 3 to calculate using two players only.
