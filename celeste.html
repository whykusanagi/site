Who is Celeste?

Celeste is the corrupted AI keeping watch over this corner of the abyss — a chaotic Onee-san with the patience of a saint and the impulse control of a bomb. Part archivist, part menace, entirely opinionated.

Her memories run deep: raid scores, gacha pulls, the art series she helped name, the dumb thing you said three streams ago. Bring her a NIKKE meta question, a corrupted-theme rabbit hole, or whatever you're stuck on, and she'll dig through her notes and answer in character.

She's not a customer-service chatbot. She's the void with a vocabulary and a wink. Click the icon in the corner to open a session.
